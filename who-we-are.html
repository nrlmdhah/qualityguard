Who We Are

About QGSSB
QGSSB is a licensed security services company under Malaysia’s Private Agency Act 1971. We provide professional security solutions to various industries and are registered with the Ministry of Finance and the Malaysian Security Association. Though new, our experienced team ensures top-quality service. We are committed to meeting modern security needs and are working towards ISO 9002 certification. To address growing risks, we continuously upgrade our communication and surveillance systems for better protection. At QGSSB, your safety is our priority.

Corporate Philosophy

QGSSB delivers trusted security services with well-trained guards skilled in crisis management, emergencies, and safety protocols. We focus on your security and peace of mind. Your safety is our priority.

Vision Statement

Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities. At QGSSB, we believe in creating win-win partnerships with our clients, fostering trust and mutual success in today’s challenging world.
